About Us

Team दृष्टिकोण is a dynamic group of enthusiastic individuals dedicated to addressing real-world challenges through the innovative use of geospatial technologies. Our current project focuses on flood hazard mapping and web-based visualization for the Melamchi and Indrawati River Basins. By integrating hydrological modeling, GIS, and remote sensing, we aim to provide accurate flood risk assessments that can aid in disaster preparedness and resource management. We are always eager to engage in projects that offer practical solutions, leveraging our expertise in geospatial technology to make a meaningful impact on society.
